👩‍💻 About Me

Maya is a passionate front-end developer who combines clean design with deep environmental values. Outside of coding, she actively volunteers in reforestation programs and biodiversity education initiatives across Sri Lanka. Her projects reflect her core belief that technology should serve both people and the planet equally — she builds accessible, eco-conscious digital solutions that make a real-world impact under the UN SDG framework.
