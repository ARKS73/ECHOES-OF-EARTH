Why It Matters

🌏

Ecosystem Balance

Over 1 million species face extinction. Healthy ecosystems underpin food, water and air for all life on Earth.

💧

Water Security

Forests act as natural water reservoirs. Protecting them guarantees clean water for 2.4 billion people worldwide.

🌡️

Climate Stability

Land ecosystems absorb 30% of global CO₂ emissions. Healthy land is our most powerful climate solution.

🤝

Human Livelihoods

Over 1.6 billion people depend on forests for food, shelter and income — making biodiversity a human rights issue.

Aligned with the UN Global Goals
